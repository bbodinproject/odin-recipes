Protein Shake

Description

An easy way to get the calories and protein in that you need to fuel an athletic lifestyle. Can easily be modified to suit your taste or macro-nutritional needs, however in general I try to aim for 750~ calories and around 50g of protein. Much more and it starts to get hard to eat. You could simply eat one twice a day if you were trying to bulk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk/yogurt or alternative
  • 1 banana
  • 2 scoops nut butter of your choice
  • 1-2 scoops protein powder of choice (you want 20-30g protein)
  • Either frozen fruit or ice cubes, something to give it a chill

Instructions

  1. Add all items to blender
  2. Blend!
  3. Enjoy! (bonus points if you drink from the blender to save dishes)
