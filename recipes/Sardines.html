Sardines

Description

One of my favorite foods, and if the Dandan noodles take too long, what could be quicker than some sardines. Extremely healthy, low in mercury like larger fish, I eat one of these tins daily for protein and the oils fish have. A longevity superfood for sure!

Ingredients

  • Can of Sardines (You will most likely want a fork too)

Instructions

  1. Remove packaging if present
  2. Carefully open tin partially to avoid spill, and drain
  3. Fully remove lid and enjoy
